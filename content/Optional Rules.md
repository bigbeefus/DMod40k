Any of the following rules are recommended to be used only if both players agree.

## Battlefield Debris

Battlefield Debris may be treated as destructible terrain with AV14 and 1HP.
# Heroic Morale

A unit that rolls heroic morale results (double 1s) may choose to immediately take a Leadership test. If it passes, it immediately loses any Pinned/Go to Ground status or may Rally if it was retreating. It may immediately perform a Reaction Order.

# Save Modifiers
*Note: The following rules are intended to be used with the Go to Ground rule changes*

Weapons inflict a modifier on armor saves based on their AP Value. Vehicle Armor remains the same.  

| **AP** | **Modifier** |
| ------ | ------------ |
| 4      | -1           |
| 3      | -2           |
| 2      | -3           |
| 1      | -4           |
## Terminator Armor (and Equivalent)
*Optional: Apply to any other models with a 2+ Armor Save and any variant of Bulky*
Grants a 1+ Save in addition to any other rules. 
Models wearing Terminator Armor may never Go to Ground
