**Scenario Type**: Attack/Defense
## SITUATION REPORT  
The enemy have already been under heavy artillery fire, and are already weakened. Spearhead an assault to break through the lines.

## TERRAIN   
Set up the terrain in any mutually agreed manner.

## VICTORY  
The player who holds all objectives after turn 5 wins the game. The game continues until all objectives are held or the other side is eliminated.

## RESERVES  
The Attacker receives Incremental Reserves starting Turn 2
The Defender receives Incremental Reserves starting Turn 3

### 1\. Determine Table Edges  
The Defender may choose which of the short table edges the Attacker must deploy on. He gets the opposite table edge. 

### 2\. Attacker’s Forces  
**Probing Force**   
The probing force includes up to a third of the Attacker's army by points plus any recon units.

**Main Force**  
The rest of the attacker’s army are the main force. From turn 2 onwards, Reserves will arrive incrementally from the attacker’s table edge at the beginning of each turn, with an additional unit every subsequent turn.

### 3\. Defender's Forces   
**Initial Defenders**
The Defender may select up to half of their points' worth units, plus any Fortifications to start. All other units are considered Defender's Reinforcements and placed in reserves.

**Defender’s Reinforcements**  
From turn 3 onwards one unit, plus one for each subsequent turn will arrive each turn from the Defender's table edge, until all units are on the tabletop.

### 4\. Objective Placement
The defender places three objectives in the Front Line Zone, and one anywhere else on the table, with the usual restrictions.

### 5\. Deploy Initial Defenders 
The Defender places any number of their initial units in the center Front Line Zone along with any Fortifications. Any units may start with a reaction order if they could receive one.

### 6\. Deploy Attacker’s Probing Force  
The Attacker’s probing force units are placed within 12" of the attacker’s table edge. 

### 7\. Deploy Remaining Defenders 
Any Initial Defenders that were not deployed previously may be set up anywhere on the Defender's half of the map. These may not be units from the Defender's reinforcements.

### 8\. Resolve Bombardment  
Each of the Defender’s non-vehicle units must take a Pinning test. Vehicles must pass a leadership test at Ld 10, or count as Crew Stunned at the start of the game.

### 9\. First Turn  
The Attacker takes the first turn.
