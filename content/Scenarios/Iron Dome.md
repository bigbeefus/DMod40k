## SITUATION REPORT  
The Defenders have dug in deep on a heavily fortified hardpoint. Advanced point defense systems have made any Initial attempts at bombarding unsatisfactory. Super-heavy support assets have been authorized for use.

The Attacker must have a single Lord of War selection, regardless of the point level or any other restrictions, and may be taken as an additional Force Organization slot with no prerequisites.

The Defender has control of an array of four Iron Dome turrets and a Command Bunker that may be placed anywhere on the Defender's half of the table as Fortifications. The Defender may not select any Lords of War for this scenario.

**Command Bunker**  
The Command Bunker is a standard Medium building (Armour Value 14 and 4 Hull Points)
with battlements. It is equipped a single Void Shield. 

**Iron Dome Turrets**  
The Defender may place up to 4 Iron Dome Turrets. These are advanced immobile point-defense cannons capable of intercepting any enemy rockets, artillery, and mortars.

## TERRAIN   
Set up the terrain in any mutually agreeable manner. 
## VICTORY  
The Attacker must destroy the Defender's Command Bunker while preserving their key asset. The Defender must defend the Command Bunker at all costs.

If the Command Bunker is destroyed, the Attacker immediately wins the game.

If the Attacker's Lord of War is destroyed, all other units immediately make a Morale test. Any unit that fails is immediately removed from play, fleeing the map and abandoning the operation.

This test must be made again with each subsequent activation order, with a -1 modifier applied for each subsequent turn.
## RESERVES  
The Attacker receives Incremental Reserves starting Turn 1
The Defender receives Incremental Reserves starting Turn 2

## SETUP
### 1\. Determine Table Edges  
The Defender may choose which of the short table edges the Attacker must deploy on. He gets the opposite table edge. 
### 2\. Attacker’s Forces  
**Escort Force**  
The Escort force includes up to a third of the Attacker's army by points plus any Recon units.

**Lord of War**  
The Attacker's selected Lord of War choice must arrive as their first Reserves choice, entering from anywhere on the Attacker's Table Edge.

**Supporting Force**  
The rest of the attacker’s army are the Supporting Force. Reserves will arrive incrementally from the attacker’s table edge at the beginning of each turn, with an additional unit every subsequent turn. 

### 3\. Defender's Forces   
**Initial Defenders**  
The Defender may select up to half of their points' worth units, plus any Fortifications to start. All other units are considered Defender's Reinforcements and placed in reserves.

**Defender’s Reinforcements**  
From turn 2 onwards one unit, plus one for each subsequent turn will arrive each turn from the Defender's table edge, until all units are on the tabletop.

### 4\. Deploy Initial Defenders 
The Defender places any number of their Initial Defenders in the center Front Line Zone along with any Fortifications. Any units that could receive one may start on a Reaction order.

### 5\. Deploy Attacker’s Escort Force  
The Attacker’s probing force units are placed within 12" of the attacker’s table edge. 

### 6\. Deploy Remaining Defenders 
Any Initial Defenders that were not deployed previously may be set up anywhere on the  half of the Defender's deployment zone closest to that player's table edge. These may not be units from the Defender's reinforcements, and they do not start on .

The Attacker takes the first turn.
