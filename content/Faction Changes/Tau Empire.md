# Tau Empire

## Special Rules
**Supporting Fire**: When an enemy unit declares a charge, all friendly models with this special rule in units within 6" of the charging unit’s target may choose to fire at the charging unit.
Models making a shooting attack this way may not be fired in the next turn, but the firing model may fire a different weapon if it has one.

## Wargear

**Bonding Knife Ritual**: Confers +1WS in addition to Heroic Morale
