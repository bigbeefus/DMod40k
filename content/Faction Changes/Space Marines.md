### Astartes Tank Commander
When adding an Astartes Tank Commander to your army, you must also take a single Tank unit from the same faction, paying the points cost listed on that vehicle’s datasheet as well as the 50 points for the Astartes Tank Commander. The chosen vehicle is a character and has Ballistic Skill 5. In addition, it has the It Will Not Die special rule and gains the Extra Armor upgrade for free.

If the tank is Wrecked or suffers an Explodes! result, the Tank Commander is treated as a passenger. (Note that if the vehicle does not have an Access Point, he will have to perform an emergency disembarkation). Assuming the Tank Commander survives his vehicle’s destruction, he uses his own characteristic profile and special rules as normal from that point on.
