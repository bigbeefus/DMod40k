---
title: Warhammer 40000 Dan's Mod
---

The following is designed to amend any edition of the Warhammer 40k rules. 

Note: The scenarios in this module are intended to be determined before list creation.

These rules are written with 7th edition in mind, but are intentionally edition-agnostic where possible. Profiles and mechanics from any edition may be used with minimal alteration in many cases.

The usual movement, shooting, assault phases are replaced with an interactive order-based activation system. Voice of Command (Imperial Guard) orders are their own still, this module will only refer to them where explicitly stated.
