# Auto Weapons
Autocannons, Quad Guns, and any other type of Faction-specific Autocannon may elect to fire using one of the following modes

**AP**: Gains Rending (6+)
**HE**: Halve the total number of shots to fire an AP6 Small Blast at -3 Strength.

**Power Sword**: Rending

**Lightning Claws**: Rending

**Skyfire**: May fire full BS at any target.
