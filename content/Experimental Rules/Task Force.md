The following rules are intended for games less than 1000 points.

# Task Force Detachment
Squads of specialists trained for tactical operations in the 41st millenium may be deployed in smaller sections allowing for greater flexibility.

# Effects
    Units that did not perform a reaction order this turn may make a tactical action at any point during their activation.
    Each unit may nominate up to two separate, appropriately modeled squad members as specialists with the following wargear for free.
        CLIMBING KIT, BREACHING CHARGES, BLIND GRENADES

# Limitations
    1 HQ MAX    
    Duplicate unit types may not be selected outside of platoons.
    Units with 2+ armor may not be selected.
    Vehicles with armor greater than 12 may not be taken.

# Tactical Actions
## Breakdown
A unit may break down into fireteams of at least 2 models each.
Fireteams are treated as separate units for the rest of the game for everything except scoring.
Fireteams automatically reform/merge by re-entering coherency with the original unit, and must spend another BREAKDOWN action to split again.
A unit may not split into more than three fireteams.
This action may be performed during the deployment phase. Fireteams from the same unit must deploy within 24" of each other.

## Rally
The unit takes a Leadership test. If the unit passes, remove 1 level of suppression.
## Breach
Creates a 2" passageway through an impassable wall up to 1" thick.


Crew Served: A friendly model from the same unit in base contact may help crew a Heavy weapon instead of firing its own.
The model firing the heavy weapon may re-roll 1 To-Hit Roll when making a ranged attack. Team weapons with full wounds gain this benefit automatically.
