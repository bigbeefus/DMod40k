# Entering the Battlefield
Flyers may may enter battlefield at any point on the owning player's table half, up to the center point of the table, unless noted otherwise.
# Shooting at Zooming Flyers
Any shooting attacks are subject to the following modifiers when shooting at a zooming flyer.
## High-Speed Dirt
*Supersonic targets are notoriously hard to hit, but a proper strafe leaves little room for maneuvers*

Any shooting attacks that target a Flyer's front arc gain +1 to hit zooming Flyers, including snapshots.

Flyers gain a 5+ cover save from ground targets in the side and rear arcs.
## Pintle-Mounted Weapons
Pintle-mounted weapons are installed for Anti-air defense roles, and can often be effective at disrupting sensitive instrumentation and other finely tuned controls.

Pintle-mounted on vehicles may always elect to target a Flyer separately, and gain Rending (6+) against Flyers (subject to other shooting restrictions).
