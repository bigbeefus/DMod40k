When a player places an objective, that player may choose to place an operational objective. A player may not choose a given objective type more than once, unless noted otherwise.

Each player may place up to 6" of surrounding Barricades or similar cover within 6" of each objective.

Once an objective has been captured, it remains in that player's control until captured or contested by an opponent's unit.

Operational Objectives may be captured and denied by entering base contact with an agreed-upon model, and otherwise use the standard capture and denial radius for objectives from a noted centerpoint on the model.

## Ammunition Cache
A unit controlling this objective re-rolls failed To Hit rolls when shooting.

**Restock**: Any model within 3" of this objective may regain a single spent One Use Only weapon instead of moving this turn. That model may not make any further voluntary movement this turn.

## Skyfire Nexus
A unit that controls this objective may choose whether or not all of the models in it have the Skyfire special rule each time they shoot.

## Communications Relay
A player that controls a Communications Relay may re-roll a single failed communications test each turn.

## Promethium Pipeline Node
A minimum of 1 short Promethium Relay Pipe section must be placed. The placing player may choose to place up to 12" of other pipeline within 6" instead of barricades. Pipes use the rules for  Fuel Pipes and Fuel Siphon.

## Plasma Reactor
**Plasma Feed**: Any plasma weapon fired by a non-vehicle model within 2" of a Plasma Reactor in the Shooting phase may use the extra power to supercharge their weapon. If they do then until the end of the phase their plasma weapon gains the Blast special rule (weapons that already have the Blast special rule gain the Large Blast special rule instead), however, their weapon also Gets Hot! on rolls of a 1 or 2, instead of just 2.

## Armored Container Cache
1-3 Munitorum Armoured Containers using the Mysterious Containers rule. Each container should be placed so that they are approximately within a 6" radius.

Bonuses for the *Uplifting Primers* result may be applied to the faction that discovered it instead, rather than Armies of the Imperium.

## Void Shield Projector
Small void shield projector that projects a single void shield within a 6" radius.

## Gun Emplacement
A Single Gun Emplacement may be chosen from the following:
- Quad-Gun
- Icarus Lascannon
- Vanquisher Battle Cannon

If both players agree, Codex-specific equivalent weapon profiles may be used instead.

## Repair Station
*Archaeotech nanite projectors or simply a tent full of a Mek's grots.*

Vehicles within 3" of a repair station may attempt to restore a hull point, Weapon Destroyed, or Immobilized result on a 4+ instead of moving.
